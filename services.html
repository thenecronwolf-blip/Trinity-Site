Our Services

Whole-person, recovery-oriented care designed to support lasting change.

Service Offerings

Addiction & Behavioral Health Counseling

Individual and group counseling services that support mental health, substance use recovery, and emotional well-being.

Peer Recovery Support

Guidance and encouragement from trained peers with lived recovery experience who understand the journey firsthand.

Pre-Job Training

Support in developing workplace readiness, communication skills, and confidence for employment success.

Skills Development

Education and life-skills training that promote independence, stability, and personal growth.

Housing Support

Assistance connecting individuals to safe, stable housing resources that support long-term recovery.

Family & Community Education

Educational programs that strengthen families and increase awareness around recovery, wellness, and prevention.
