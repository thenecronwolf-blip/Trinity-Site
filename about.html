About Trinity Care Services

Empowering individuals, supporting families, and strengthening communities through compassionate care and evidence-based programs.

Our Mission

At Trinity Care Services, our mission is to provide holistic, client-centered care to individuals battling substance use and behavioral health challenges. We believe that with the right support, every individual has the capacity to heal, grow, and thrive.

Our History

Founded in 2019 by Marvina Thomas, a former registered nurse with over two decades of experience in health and community service, Trinity Care Services was born from a vision of compassionate care. Over the years, we have expanded to provide comprehensive behavioral health counseling, pre-job training, and housing support for those who need it most.
