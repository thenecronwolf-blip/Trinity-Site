Housing & Insurance Support

Providing safe, stable housing and access to the right medical coverage for every client.

Safe and Supportive Housing

We partner with local housing programs and shelters to provide temporary and long-term housing solutions for clients in recovery. Our goal is to ensure a safe environment conducive to healing and personal growth.

Group Homes

Structured living with support staff on-site to assist with recovery and daily life skills.

Transitional Housing

Short-term housing to bridge the gap between treatment and independent living.

Permanent Housing Support

Guidance on finding long-term housing and navigating rental agreements.

Insurance Navigation

Understanding insurance options can be overwhelming. Trinity Care Services helps clients navigate Medicaid, private insurance, and other programs to ensure access to vital behavioral health services.

Medicaid Assistance

Enroll and maintain Medicaid benefits with our guidance, ensuring coverage for treatment programs.

Private Insurance Support

We help clients maximize private insurance coverage for therapy, counseling, and medical care.

Financial Counseling

Personalized sessions to help clients understand costs, coverage, and options for care.
