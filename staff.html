Marvina Thomas, BA

Founder and Owner

A former registered nurse, Marvina is the Founder and Owner of Trinity Care Services. She moved to the Phoenix area 25 years ago and began her mission in being a ministry of health. Marvina has been helping the homeless via her group homes, Start Living Inc., since 2003 and opened Trinity Care Services in 2019 to further help those battling substance abuse.
