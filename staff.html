Meet Our Staff

Our team is committed to providing compassionate, evidence-based care that supports recovery, stability, and long-term wellness.

Marvina Thomas, BA

Founder & Owner

Marvina Thomas is the Founder and Owner of Trinity Care Services. A former registered nurse, she relocated to the Phoenix area over 25 years ago and has dedicated her life to serving individuals through health-focused ministry.

Since 2003, Marvina has supported individuals experiencing homelessness through her group homes, Start Living Inc. In 2019, she established Trinity Care Services to expand access to behavioral health and substance abuse treatment.

Contact Marvina

Marcia Chowdhury, MSAC

Master Level Clinician

Marcia Chowdhury earned both her Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Addiction Counseling from Ottawa University.

As a Master Level Clinician, she provides oversight and supervision to counselors and case managers, ensuring treatment plans are individualized, ethical, and clinically sound.

Errol Horn, BA

Life Skills Facilitator

Errol Horn serves as the Life Skills Facilitator at Trinity Care Services and brings over 15 years of experience in behavioral health and recovery.

With a background in psychology from Alabama A&M University, Errol integrates cognitive psychology into his groups to help clients build resilience, accountability, and healthy coping strategies.
