Meet Our Staff

Marvina Thomas, BA

Founder and Owner

A former registered nurse, Marvina is the Founder and Owner of Trinity Care Services. She moved to the Phoenix area over 25 years ago and began her mission as a ministry of health. Marvina has supported individuals experiencing homelessness through her group homes, Start Living Inc., since 2003, and founded Trinity Care Services in 2019 to further serve those battling substance abuse.

Marcia Chowdhury, MSAC

Master Level Clinician

Ms. Chowdhury obtained both her Master’s and Bachelor’s degrees in Addiction Counseling from Ottawa University. As a Clinical Supervisor, she oversees counselors and case managers, ensuring the development and implementation of individualized, effective treatment plans rooted in best practices.

Errol Horn, BA

Facilitator

Errol is the LifeSkills Facilitator at Trinity Care Services, bringing over 15 years of experience in behavioral health and recovery. He incorporates principles of cognitive psychology into his sessions and holds a degree in Psychology from Alabama A&M University. Errol is currently working on a non-fiction book focused on life experience, trauma, and personal growth.

Ashley Coleman, BSN, RN

Registered Nurse

An Arizona native and proud military spouse, Ashley is a Registered Nurse at Trinity Care Services. Her background includes caring for medically fragile adults and hospice patients, as well as experience in cardiac and mother-baby nursing. She is deeply committed to integrating mental health services into comprehensive medical care.
